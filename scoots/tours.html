Tours

Here are some fun things to do in cozumel

Palancar Reef

For those sporty enough enjoy some fierce volleyball competition on the beaches of cozumel! It is one of the best ways to enjoy the trip and make lasting friendships in the process. Be sure to enjoy sand volleyball at the beaches of one of the most beautiful places in the world.

Cozumel's Beaches

Among the most popular and attractive of Cozumel's beaches are Playa San Juan and Playa Santa Pilar in the northwest corner of the island, and Playa San Francisco, Playa Santa Rosa, and Playa Palancar to the southwest.

Snorkeling in Palancar

Whether you are an experienced snorkeler or a total noob, expert guides let you make the most of Cozumels famous reefs. The most popular spots are Palancar,Columbia, and Playa el Cielo

ATV Mud Kicking

Get off road in the Cozumel jungles with an all terrain vehicle tour. Follow expert guides through the jungle to the Jade canote, where you can take dip and cool off!

Mayan Bee Sanctuary is a place to discover the stingless bee and Melipona honey. Tour the sacntuary and discover many ruines, sculptures, and see behind the scnenes of one of the largest operators of tourism in Mexico.

Dolphin Experiences

Cozumel is home to many Dolphin experiences and businesses. You will be able to swim and hang out with the lovely Dolphins and enjoy a magical experience with this marine species in one of the most beautiful places in the world.

Atlantis Submarines

Experience a dive with Atlantis Submarines. During the dive, you will visit the Felipe Xicotencatl wreck that sank in the year 2000 plus colourful schools of fish, sharks, turtles, stingrays and lobsters.

Eat at Local Restaurants

Eating street food is one of the best things to do in Cozumel and Mexican causin is particularly delicious! Be sure to try a variety of foods and to explore the different flavors that the island has to offer.

Deep-Sea Fishing

For those who enjoy deep sea fishing Cozumel is the perfect place! Take advantage of the different deals and packages offered and prepare for an amazing fishing experience in some clear clean water!.
